Prepare yourself for Aptitude

06 June 2022

Why IT companies take Aptitude Tests?

An aptitude test is a structured assessment that aims to evaluate job candidates or even existing employees talent and skill in completing certain tasks without any prior knowledge or training. There are lots of different types of aptitude test which can assess a series of core skills. An increasing number of employers are now using aptitude tests as a way to assess the specific capabilities of potential employees. They are often used as a method of screening to identify high calibre candidates. The content of an aptitude test, particularly in an industry such as innovation, needs careful thought and consideration. They are used to assess candidates in terms of their skills, knowledge, ability and personality. Aptitude tests are an accurate way of predicting the potential contribution of a candidate and the likelihood of them achieving success within the organization. It can sometimes be difficult from an interview alone to assess the candidate's skills, so an aptitude test provides the employer with a greater insight into their capabilities. So, without a good preparation of aptitude you will not be able to crack the job opportunity.

Types of Aptitude

Different types of Aptitude tests are there. But IT firms take Aptitude tests on some specific topics. They are Numerical Aptitude, Reasoning Aptitude, Verbal Aptitude, Spatial Aptitude (considered under Reasoning), and Technical Aptitude. We will discuss each in this blog.

Numerical Aptitude

The numerical aptitude section consists of different questions from different mathematical topics. Any higher mathematics questions is not there. Secondary standard math questions are there; like - time, speed and distance, time and work, etc. If you solve these numerical problems by the conventional method, then it may take a very long time. So, what you need is to practice them in a daily manner; so that your speed will be increased. Along with it, you have to remember several short tricks. Go topic by topic, remember short tricks for each topic, and practice a lot of problems from each topic - this should be your study plan.

Reasoning Aptitude

After numerical aptitude the major part of the aptitude test consists of reasoning. There are different kinds of topics which comes under reasoning. Coding-decoding, syllogism, seating arrangement, etc. You may face difficulty in some topics like syllogism, blood relation, etc. So you need a lot of practice. Check below to get all the topics related to reasoning.

  • Reasoning Aptitude Topics

Verbal Aptitude

This section is most easy and students get marks easily in this part. We are learning english since childhood. So, we have a strong knowledge about english. In verbal aptitude it consists of reading comprehension, sentence correction, voice change, narattion change, etc. But one thing is you should have a rich vocabulary. Otherwise you may get stuck in synonyms and antonyms, homophones, etc.

  • Verbal Aptitude Topics

Spatial Aptitude

Sometimes, spatial aptitude is considered as a part of the reasoning aptitude. Spatial aptitude is all about 2-dimensional and 3-dimensional figures. Spatial aptitude tests how much imagination power you have. It consists of cubes, dices, paper folding, paper cutting, etc. awesome topics. It is very easy to get marks from this part.

  • Spatial Aptitude Topics

Technical Aptitude

Most of the companies take technical aptitude as a different round along with the coding questions. Though few companies like Capgemini, take technical aptitude with the other aptitudes in the first round. This technical aptitude section varies from one company to another. In most cases, technical aptitude contains pseudocode. Pseudocode is not written in any specific programming language. Pseudocode is written with a simple algorithmic syntax to understand the steps of a problem. This pseudocode can contain problems related to data structures and algorithms, programming control structures, loops, etc. Besides it, technical aptitude contains C or C++ or Java or Python related code snippet output. This section can contain multiple choice questions from data structures and algorithms, graph theory, programming fundamentals, etc.

Psychometric Aptitude

Generally, this round is conducted separately. This round is not an elimination round. Psychometric Aptitude test is taken to assess psychological behavior. This test consists of simple questions like - do you like to work in a team or not? It's an easy round but the main thing is this round consists of a lot of questions and they give you very less time to complete all these questions. But some companies do not provide time limit for psychometric test.
